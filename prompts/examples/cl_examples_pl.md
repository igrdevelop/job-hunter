# Cover Letter Examples — Polish (PL)
# 15 realistic senior-level cover letters for frontend/web engineers
# Source: curated examples for Polish/EU job market
# Usage: reference for tone, structure, length, and proof-point patterns

---

## Key principles (PL market)
- Length: 120–200 words
- Must contain: scale (users / system / team), impact (performance / revenue / bugs), role (lead / ownership / architecture)
- NO: "passion", "learning", "hardworking"
- YES: architecture, scale, business impact, leadership, ownership
