# Cover Letter Examples — English (EN)
# 15 realistic senior-level cover letters for frontend/web engineers
# Source: curated examples for global/EU/remote job market
# Usage: reference for tone, structure, length, and proof-point patterns

---

## Key principles (EN market)
- Length: 150–250 words (slightly longer than PL)
- Must contain: architecture, scale, measurable impact, ownership
- NO: "passion for technology", "eager to learn", "hardworking team player"
- YES: systems at scale, metrics, decisions made, business outcomes
